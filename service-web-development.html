Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our web development services

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically, a standard website takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with e-commerce or custom features may take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes! We can help you set up hosting and register your domain name. We work with reliable hosting providers and can manage everything for you, or guide you through the process if you prefer to manage it yourself.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach and are fully responsive. Your website will look and function perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update text, images, and content without any technical knowledge. We also provide training on how to use the CMS.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, backups, performance monitoring, and technical support. This ensures your website stays secure and up-to-date.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and various CMS platforms like WordPress, custom PHP, and Node.js. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements.

Will my website be SEO optimized?

Yes! SEO optimization is built into every website we create. This includes proper meta tags, structured data, fast loading times, mobile optimization, and clean code structure - all essential for good search engine rankings.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Absolutely! We can redesign your existing website to give it a modern look, improve functionality, and enhance user experience. We can work with your current content or help create new content.
