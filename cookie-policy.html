Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you interact with our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly, including session management and security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for a more personalised experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign performance.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and Supabase that place cookies on your device. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in the following ways:

  • Through your browser settings — most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies.
  • Using our consent banner when you first visit the site.
  • Visiting our Consent Preferences page at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at: info@trendtacticsdigital.com
