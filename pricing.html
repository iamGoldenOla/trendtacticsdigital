Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and pricing

How do I start a project with you?

Simply fill out the "Get a Custom Quote" form on this page or the contact form. We will get back to you within 24 hours to schedule a free consultation and discuss your project in detail.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely. While we have standardized tiers for web development to help you get started quickly, we specialize in custom solutions tailored to your unique business goals.

What is your payment structure?

Typically, we work with a 50% deposit to start the project and 50% upon completion/launch. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you provide ongoing technical support?

Yes, all our packages include a support period (ranging from 1 to 6 months). After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans to keep your site or app updated and secure.
