Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our email marketing services

What is included in your email marketing service?

Our email marketing service includes strategy development, email template design, content creation, list segmentation, automated email sequences, A/B testing, performance analytics, and regular campaign optimization to improve open rates and conversions.

How do you segment email lists?

We segment email lists based on various criteria including purchase history, engagement levels, demographics, interests, and behavior. This allows us to send highly targeted, relevant messages that improve engagement and conversion rates.

What email marketing platforms do you work with?

We work with all major email marketing platforms including Mailchimp, Constant Contact, SendGrid, Klaviyo, ConvertKit, and more. We can also help you choose the best platform for your business needs.

How often should I send marketing emails?

The optimal email frequency depends on your audience and industry. We'll help you determine the right frequency through testing and analysis. Generally, we recommend 1-2 emails per week for most businesses, but this can vary based on your specific goals and audience preferences.

What kind of results can I expect from email marketing?

Email marketing typically delivers strong ROI, with average open rates of 20-25% and click-through rates of 2-5%. Results vary by industry and list quality. We'll set realistic expectations based on your specific situation and work to continuously improve performance.

Do you create email content?

Yes! We create all email content including subject lines, body copy, CTAs, and design elements. All content is tailored to your brand voice and optimized for engagement and conversions.
