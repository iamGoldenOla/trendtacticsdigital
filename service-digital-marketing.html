Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our digital marketing services

What digital marketing services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive digital marketing services including Email Marketing, Content Creation, Social Media Marketing, Facebook Ads, SEO, PPC advertising, and analytics. We create integrated campaigns that work across all channels.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Results vary by channel. Paid advertising (Facebook Ads, PPC) can show results within days to weeks. SEO typically takes 3-6 months to see significant results. Email marketing and social media can show engagement improvements within weeks. We'll set realistic expectations during our consultation.

Do you manage social media accounts?

Yes! We provide full social media management services including content creation, posting schedules, community management, engagement, and strategy development across all major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and more.

What is included in your email marketing service?

Our email marketing service includes strategy development, email template design, content creation, list segmentation, automated email sequences, A/B testing, performance analytics, and regular campaign optimization to improve open rates and conversions.

How do you measure campaign success?

We track key metrics including website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, ROI, engagement rates, click-through rates, and revenue attribution. You'll receive monthly reports with detailed analytics and insights on campaign performance.

Do you create content for our marketing campaigns?

Absolutely! Our content creation services include blog posts, articles, social media posts, email content, video scripts, infographics, and more. All content is tailored to your brand voice and optimized for your target audience.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is organic, long-term strategy to improve your website's visibility in search results. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is paid advertising that shows immediate results. We typically recommend using both for maximum visibility and results.

Can you help with Facebook and Instagram advertising?

Yes! We specialize in Facebook and Instagram advertising. We create targeted ad campaigns, design compelling ad creatives, manage ad budgets, optimize for conversions, and provide detailed reporting on ad performance and ROI.
