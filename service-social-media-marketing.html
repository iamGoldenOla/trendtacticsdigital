Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our social media marketing services

Which social media platforms do you manage?

We manage all major social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, and Pinterest. We'll help you determine which platforms are best for your business and create strategies for each.

How often do you post on social media?

Posting frequency depends on the platform and your industry. Generally, we recommend 3-5 posts per week on Facebook, 1-2 posts per day on Instagram, and multiple tweets per day on Twitter. We'll create a customized posting schedule based on your goals and audience.

Do you create all the content for social media?

Yes! We create all social media content including posts, graphics, captions, stories, and videos. All content is tailored to your brand voice and optimized for each platform's best practices.

How do you measure social media success?

We track key metrics including follower growth, engagement rates, reach, impressions, clicks, conversions, and ROI. You'll receive monthly reports with detailed analytics and insights on your social media performance.

Can you help with social media advertising?

Yes! We create and manage social media advertising campaigns across all platforms. This includes Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and Twitter Ads. We optimize campaigns for maximum ROI and conversions.

How long does it take to see results from social media marketing?

You can start seeing engagement improvements within the first few weeks. Follower growth and brand awareness typically take 2-3 months to show significant results. We'll set realistic expectations based on your starting point and goals.
