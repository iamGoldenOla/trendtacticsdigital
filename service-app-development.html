Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our app development services

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

Simple apps typically take 2-3 months, while more complex apps with advanced features can take 4-6 months or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your app's requirements and features.

Should I build a native or cross-platform app?

It depends on your needs. Native apps offer the best performance and platform-specific features, while cross-platform apps are more cost-effective and faster to develop. We'll help you choose the best approach for your project.

Do you help with app store submission?

Yes! We handle the entire app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including preparing all required materials, screenshots, descriptions, and ensuring compliance with store guidelines.

Can you integrate third-party services and APIs?

Absolutely! We can integrate payment gateways, social media APIs, analytics tools, push notifications, cloud services, and any other third-party services your app needs to function properly.

What about app maintenance and updates?

We offer ongoing maintenance packages that include bug fixes, security updates, compatibility updates for new OS versions, feature enhancements, and technical support to keep your app running smoothly.

Do you provide app design services?

Yes! Our team includes UI/UX designers who create beautiful, user-friendly app designs. We focus on creating intuitive interfaces that provide excellent user experiences and drive engagement.

Can you build apps for both iOS and Android?

Yes! We develop apps for both platforms. You can choose native development for each platform or cross-platform development (React Native/Flutter) to build for both platforms simultaneously, saving time and cost.

What technologies do you use for app development?

We use modern technologies including Swift and SwiftUI for iOS, Kotlin for Android, React Native and Flutter for cross-platform development, along with various backend technologies and cloud services as needed.
