Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Facebook Ads services

How much should I budget for Facebook Ads?

Facebook ad budgets vary based on your goals and industry. We typically recommend starting with a minimum of $500-1000 per month for testing and optimization. Larger budgets allow for better scaling and results. We'll help you determine the right budget based on your objectives.

How long does it take to see results from Facebook Ads?

You can start seeing results within days of launching campaigns. However, it typically takes 1-2 weeks for Facebook's algorithm to optimize and find your best-performing audiences. We continuously optimize campaigns to improve performance over time.

What types of Facebook Ads do you create?

We create all types of Facebook and Instagram ads including image ads, video ads, carousel ads, collection ads, Stories ads, and Reels ads. We choose the best ad format based on your goals and what resonates with your target audience.

Do you manage Instagram Ads as well?

Yes! Since Facebook and Instagram are part of the same advertising platform, we manage ads on both platforms. We can run campaigns on Facebook, Instagram, or both, depending on where your target audience is most active.

How do you target the right audience?

We use Facebook's advanced targeting options including demographics, interests, behaviors, and custom audiences. We also create lookalike audiences based on your existing customers and use retargeting to reach people who have visited your website or engaged with your brand.

What kind of ROI can I expect from Facebook Ads?

ROI varies by industry and campaign goals. On average, our clients see 3-5x ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) for e-commerce campaigns and strong lead generation results for B2B campaigns. We'll set realistic expectations based on your specific business and goals.
