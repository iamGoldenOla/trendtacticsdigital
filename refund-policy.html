Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Our Commitment: We are committed to delivering exceptional value. If you are not satisfied with our services, we want to hear from you so we can make it right.

1. Service-Based Refunds

As a digital services agency, most of our work is custom and project-based. Our refund eligibility depends on the stage of the project:

  • Before project commencement: Full refund of any deposit paid if cancellation occurs within 48 hours of booking.
  • After project start (within 7 days): Partial refund (up to 50%) at our discretion, minus work already completed.
  • After 7 days or upon project completion: No refund is applicable as services have been rendered in good faith.

2. Retainer & Monthly Services

For ongoing retainer agreements (e.g., monthly SEO, social media management):

  • You may cancel your retainer with 30 days written notice.
  • The current month's fee is non-refundable if work has already begun.
  • Prepaid periods beyond the current month will be refunded on a pro-rata basis.

3. Digital Products & Ebooks

Due to the digital nature of our products (ebooks, templates, downloadable assets), all sales are final. However, if a product is faulty or not as described, we will offer a replacement or full refund.

4. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, contact us within the applicable timeframe:

  • Email: info@trendtacticsdigital.com
  • Include your name, project details, and reason for the refund request.
  • We will respond within 5 business days.

5. Contact Us

For any questions about this policy, please contact our team directly.
